Conference Introduction

The 2015 International Forum of IoT and Applications (IF[IoT&A]) will be held on 26th -27th November 2015, in Wuhan hosted by Wuhan University of Technology.

The 2015 International Forum of the IoT and Applications aims to bring together academics, scientists, engineers and postgraduate students to exchange and share their knowledge and research results on all aspects of Internet of Things (IoT) and to discuss practical challenges and solutions.

The forum will take place in the form of invited speeches, oral and poster presentations, and exhibitions. There will be over 20 invited talks at the forum from world leading researchers and scientists worldwide including USA, Europe and China. In addition to these, prospective authors are invited to submit original contributions on their latest research findings. Only one page extended abstract is required for submission to the forum. Conference proceedings will be produced from the submissions.

After the forum, the authors are invited to submit their full papers, to special issues in (1) Transactions of the Institute of Measurement and Control, and (2) Journal of Connectivity: Exploring the Internet of Things, or to relevant journals of IET, which will be subject to a normal review process of the journals. The forum organising committee does not guarantee that a full paper will be accepted for publication. Only those full papers meeting the requirements of the respective journals will be published.
