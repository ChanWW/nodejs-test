Hotel Information


The main hotel associated with the international forum is Future City Hotel, which is about 5 minutes walking distance from the conference venue.

The address of the Future City Hotel is Luoshi South Road, Hongshan Distric, Wuhan, China (武汉市武昌洪山区街道口珞狮南路). Other details can be found at http://www.futurecity-hotel.com.

The hotel can be reached by taxi from the Wuhan International Airport (Wuhan Airport) or all three train stations in Wuhan. Other hotels may also be found near the conference venue.
